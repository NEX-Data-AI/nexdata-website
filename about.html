Meet the Team

Donnie Arbeau

Co-Founder & CEO

With over 20 years in GIS, spatial intelligence, and regional planning, Donnie brings deep expertise in federal compliance, grant management, infrastructure mapping, and geospatial automation. Former GIS Manager at the Central Florida Regional Planning Council, he has built GIS programs from the ground up, launched interactive mapping platforms, and developed tools for 911 dispatch systems.
